When we created E-Generator,

we wanted to design a user-friendly way for our customers and business partners to create their own engineering reports and take control of their schedules. We believe we’ve succeeded.

Upon entering basic project information into this online engineering report generator, it determines seismic and wind engineering calculations, including anchorage selections. It also selects VMC Group products (if required) and generates a PDF report containing order and installation information plus a complete engineering calculation package – including PE Stamp!

E-Generator also allows for one-click requests for PE-stamping by state and emails you when the report has been approved or rejected within 24 hours. And once you’ve sent the selections and stamp, you can order the VMC Group products required for that report.

IBC, ASCE7, AISC and
ACI Calculations

E-Generator produces IBC, ASCE7, AISC, ACI code-compliant calculations (required by law per state) for the attachments above and below the isolator.

The requiredcalculations and checks by codes and standards, require several simple physics simulations that necessitate minimum information from users, but offers maximum accuracy in results.

The e-generator’s

end isolator selection is based on minimizing total cost (material and labor) for each project. That means if fifteen different isolators could work on an installation, E-Generator will pick the least expensive.

IBC:

Provides maps for seismic/ wind requirements on equipment

ASCE7:

Provides equations to turn statistical maps into actual forces onto the equipment

AISC:

Provides requirements specifically for steel-bolts/ steel-weld connections

ACI:

Provides requirements specifically for concrete-anchorage
